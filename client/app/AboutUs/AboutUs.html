About Us

Your Fund’s mission is to help investors achieve their financial goals by giving them the knowledge and tools they need to build and manage their own individual funds.

Your Fund was created when we asked ourselves this question: If mutual fund managers can create a diversified portfolio of stocks and bonds, why can’t an average investor do the same?

Your Fund was designed by Neal Steiner, MBA, a Personal Financial Planner and investor with over 25 years of investing experience. Steiner posed the above question in a Series 7 broker licensing course, but neither his instructor nor his classmates could come up with a satisfactory answer. Now, Steiner and his team have done just that: created a way for the average investor to design and build an affordable, diversified portfolio customized to his or her needs.

With Your Fund, individual investors can become better educated about investing and design and build their own affordable, diversified portfolio that can be tailored to their own personal goals.

Whether you are a seasoned investor or just getting started, Your Fund can help you learn about investing and create customized funds that are tailored to your short-term and long-term financial goals.
