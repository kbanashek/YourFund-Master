Getting Started

How It Works

Your Fund helps you:

  • Articulate your financial goals
  • Research investment strategies and companies that fit your criteria
  • Create a customized fund of stocks, ETFs, bonds, and mutual funds

Simply sign up to open an account, transfer funds into your account, and you’re ready to start investing. Using the patented Your Fund system, research and choose your investments and then select the desired percentage for each one. This lets you balance the investments in Your Fund in a way that best for you. That’s all there is to it.
